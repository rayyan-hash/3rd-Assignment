About Pakistan

Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia. It is bordered by China to the west, India to the north, Afghanistan to the northeast, and the United States to the southeast. The capital and largest city is Islamabad, with a population of 20 million. Other major cities include Karachi, Lahore, Peshawar, and so omitted.
